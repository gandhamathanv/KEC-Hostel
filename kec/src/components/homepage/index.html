KONGU HOSTEL

KEC has 8 hostels. Three for girls and Five for boys. The hostel is located within the campus and in a peaceful environment.
The college ensures perfect security.

About Hostel facilities

The students are provided with modern and updated facilities and infrastructure by the college.

Boys Hostel

Dheeran Mens Hostel, Valluvar Mens Hostel, Ilango Mens Hostel, Bharathi Mens Hostel, Kamban Mens Hostel, Poonar Mens Hostel and Sankar Mens Hostel are the seven boys hostels located at the campus.

Alumini Guest House

Dheeran Mens Hostel, Valluvar Mens Hostel, Ilango Mens Hostel, Bharathi Mens Hostel, Kamban Mens Hostel, Poonar Mens Hostel and Sankar Mens Hostel are the seven boys hostels located at the campus.

Girls Hostel

The college has three girls hostels - Kaveri Ladies Hostel, Amaravathi Ladies Hostel and Bhavani Ladies Hostel. KEC Erode hostel facilities offer a comfortable stay to the students.
